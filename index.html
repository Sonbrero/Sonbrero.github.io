Drinks


1. Vodka Tonic


Ingredients:
2 oz vodka
Tonic Water
Lime wedge
Instructions: Fill a glass with ice, pour in vodka, top with tonic water, and garnish with a lime wedge.



2. Classic Vodka Martini


Ingredients:
2 ½ oz vodka
½ oz dry vermouth
Lemon twist or olive
Instructions: Shake vodka and vermouth with ice, strain into a martini glass, and garnish with a lemon twist or olive.



3. Moscow Mule


Ingredients:
2 oz vodka
½ oz lime juice
Ginger beer
Lime wedge
Instructions: Pour vodka and lime juice into a mug filled with ice, top with ginger beer, and garnish with a lime wedge.



4. Screwdriver


Ingredients:
2 oz vodka
4 oz orange juice
Orange slice (optional)
Instructions: Pour vodka into a glass with ice, add orange juice, stir, and garnish with an orange slice.



5. Cosmopolitan


Ingredients:
1 ½ oz vodka
1 oz cranberry juice
½ oz lime juice
½ oz triple sec
Instructions: Shake all ingredients with ice, strain into a cocktail glass, and garnish with a lime twist.



6. Bloody Mary


Ingredients:
2 oz vodka
4 oz tomato juice
½ oz lemon juice
A dash of hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper
Celery stick for garnish
Instructions: Mix all ingredients in a glass with ice, stir, and garnish with a celery stick.



7. Cape Codder


Ingredients:
2 oz vodka
3 oz cranberry juice
Lime wedge
Instructions: Fill a glass with ice, pour vodka and cranberry juice, stir, and garnish with a lime wedge.



8. Vodka Lemonade


Ingredients:
2 oz vodka
4 oz lemonade
Lemon slice (optional)
Instructions: Pour vodka into a glass with ice, add lemonade, stir, and garnish with a lemon slice.



9. Vodka Collins


Ingredients:
2 oz vodka
1 oz lemon juice
½ oz simple syrup
Soda water
Instructions: Mix vodka, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a glass with ice. Top with soda water and stir.



10. Blue Lagoon


Ingredients:
1 ½ oz vodka
1 oz blue curaçao
Lemonade
Instructions: Pour vodka and blue curaçao into a glass filled with ice. Top with lemonade and stir. Garnish with a lemon slice.
